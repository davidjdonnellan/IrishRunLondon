Meet the organisers

Whether it's your first week, or you're here every week, the organisers are on hand to make you feel welcome Each week you'll find one of us leading the runs, and usually one of us at the back for anyone wanting a relaxed chatty pace. We're on hand to make the runs as comfortable and as smooth as possible. We're so delighted with how popular our runs are becoming.
